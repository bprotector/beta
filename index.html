Welcome to BProtector

Secure your files on the Solana blockchain with advanced encryption and steganography.

Supported Extensions

txt, pdf, jpg, jpeg, png, mp4, mp3, rtf, doc, docx, zip, tar, rar, exe, gz, 7z, bz2, iso, apk, jar

How BProtector Works

1. Connect Wallet

Connect your Phantom wallet on Solana to securely interact with the blockchain.

2. Protect Files

Upload a cover file (e.g., .jpg) and a confidential file (e.g., .txt), encrypt them, and use Solana blockchain to protect your assets.

3. Decrypt and Recover Files

Retrieve your data from Solana, decrypt your files, and download the original content.
